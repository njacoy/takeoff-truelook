## What you've actually got

Your site isn't a template or a drag-and-drop builder — it's **custom-built from the ground up**. That's why it looks and moves the way it does, loads fast, and feels nothing like everyone else's site.

The tradeoff (and it's a fair one to know about): because it's hand-built, there's no "log in and edit" dashboard like Squarespace or Wix. The upside is a site that's genuinely yours, faster, and award-quality. The flip side is that changes come through me rather than a self-serve editor. More on how that works below — it's easy.

## Can you edit it yourself?

Short version: **the content side, sometimes. The design and animation side, no — and that's by design.** Here's the honest breakdown:

| You can handle yourself | I handle for you |
|---|---|
| Viewing your analytics / traffic | Any text, image, or content changes* |
| Managing your domain & renewals | New sections or pages |
| Forwarding leads from your contact form | Design tweaks, layout, colors, fonts |
| Sharing the live link 😎 | Animations & interactions |
| | Anything involving the code |

\* *If we set your site up with a content editor (a "CMS"), you'll be able to update certain text and images yourself through a simple dashboard — I'll show you exactly how if that applies to your site. Most landing pages don't need this, but if yours updates often, just ask.*

First, a quick **hard refresh** (Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + R) fixes 90% of "it looks weird" moments — that's usually your browser showing an old cached version.

If something's genuinely broken, email me. **Bugs from the original build are fixed free for 30 days after launch.** After that, fixes fall under your plan or ad-hoc time.
